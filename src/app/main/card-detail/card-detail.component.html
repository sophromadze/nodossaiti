Purpose: Regular cleaning refers to routine maintenance cleaning that is scheduled regularly to maintain a desired level of cleanliness and tidiness.

Tasks: It typically includes basic tasks performed on a recurring basis, such as dusting, vacuuming, mopping floors, wiping surfaces, and cleaning bathrooms and kitchens.

Frequency: It is done on a frequent basis, often weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly, depending on the needs and preferences of the homeowner or business.
Purpose: Deep cleaning is a more thorough and intensive cleaning process aimed at tackling accumulated dirt, grime, and buildup that may not be addressed in regular cleaning sessions.

Tasks: It involves cleaning tasks that go beyond the routine, such as scrubbing tiles and grout, cleaning inside appliances (like ovens and refrigerators), washing baseboards, cleaning light fixtures, and removing dust and cobwebs from corners and high surfaces.

Frequency: It is typically done less frequently than regular cleaning and is often performed on a seasonal basis or as needed to maintain a higher level of cleanliness.
Move-In Cleaning Service: A move-in cleaning service is designed to prepare a new residence or commercial space for occupancy. It focuses on thorough cleaning to ensure the space is fresh, sanitized, and welcoming for the new occupants.

Tasks: Cleaning all surfaces, including floors, walls, and ceilings; disinfecting bathrooms and kitchens; wiping down fixtures such as cabinets, countertops, and appliances; sweeping, vacuuming, and mopping all floors; washing interior windows to remove dust and grime; removing dust from baseboards, light fixtures, and other surfaces; disposing of any leftover trash or debris.
Move-Out Cleaning Service: A move-out cleaning service is performed when occupants are vacating a property. It focuses on deep cleaning to prepare the space for new tenants or owners.

Tasks: Thoroughly cleaning all areas of the property, including bathrooms, kitchens, and living spaces; addressing any stains or marks on walls, floors, or carpets; cleaning inside and outside of appliances like refrigerators and ovens; washing windows and cleaning blinds or curtains; wiping down baseboards, door frames, and trim; vacuuming and spot-treating carpets to remove dirt and stains; ensuring all areas are spotless and ready for the next occupants.
