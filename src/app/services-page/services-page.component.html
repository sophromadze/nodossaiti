Regular Cleaning

Regular cleaning is an essential service designed to keep your home or office environment consistently clean, comfortable, and inviting. This service is ideal for those who wish to maintain a high level of cleanliness in their space on a frequent basis. Regular cleaning typically includes a comprehensive range of tasks such as dusting furniture and fixtures, vacuuming carpets and rugs, mopping hard floors, wiping down all accessible surfaces, and thoroughly cleaning the kitchen and bathroom areas. In the kitchen, regular cleaning covers tasks like cleaning the sink, countertops, and exterior surfaces of appliances, as well as wiping down cabinet doors and sweeping and mopping the floors. In the bathroom, regular cleaning involves disinfecting and scrubbing the toilet, sink, and shower or bathtub, as well as cleaning mirrors and floors. This service ensures that your living or working space remains fresh, hygienic, and clutter-free, providing a pleasant atmosphere for you, your family, or your employees.

Deep Cleaning

Deep cleaning is a specialized and exhaustive cleaning service that goes far beyond the scope of regular cleaning. It is designed for spaces that require a more intensive clean, typically after a prolonged period of neglect or before or after major events or seasonal changes. Deep cleaning involves a meticulous approach to cleaning every nook and cranny of your home or office, including those areas that are often overlooked during regular cleaning. This service typically includes scrubbing and sanitizing the interior and exterior of kitchen appliances like ovens, refrigerators, and microwaves; cleaning inside cupboards and drawers; washing baseboards, door frames, and light fixtures; deep cleaning of tiles and grout in bathrooms and kitchens; and thorough dusting and cleaning of hard-to-reach areas like ceiling fans, vents, and behind furniture. In addition, deep cleaning can involve specialized tasks such as stain removal from carpets and upholstery, and intensive cleaning of windows, blinds, and window sills. This service is ideal for homeowners who want to refresh their living spaces or prepare their homes for guests, as well as for businesses looking to present a pristine and professional environment to clients and employees.

Move In Service

Move In cleaning is an essential service designed to ensure that your new home is not only spotless but also ready for you and your family to settle in with peace of mind. This service is especially important when moving into a previously occupied home or apartment, where a thorough clean is necessary to remove any dirt, dust, or allergens left behind by previous occupants. Move In cleaning covers every corner of your new home, from the bedrooms and living areas to the kitchen, bathrooms, and storage spaces. The service includes detailed cleaning of all surfaces, such as scrubbing and sanitizing kitchen countertops, sinks, and fixtures, cleaning inside and outside of all appliances like ovens and refrigerators, and wiping down the interiors and exteriors of cabinets and drawers. In bathrooms, the service involves deep cleaning of showers, bathtubs, toilets, sinks, and mirrors, ensuring that all fixtures are disinfected and sparkling. Floors throughout the home are thoroughly vacuumed and mopped, and all windows, windowsills, and doors are cleaned. Move In cleaning gives you the confidence that your new living space is clean, fresh, and ready for you to make it your own.

Move Out Service

Move Out cleaning is an indispensable service that helps ensure you leave your old home in the best possible condition, whether you're moving out of a rental property and need to meet the terms of your lease or selling your home and want to make a great impression on potential buyers. This comprehensive cleaning service addresses every aspect of your home, ensuring that it is left in pristine condition for the next occupants. Move Out cleaning includes thoroughly cleaning and sanitizing all rooms, with special attention given to the kitchen and bathrooms. In the kitchen, all appliances are cleaned inside and out, countertops and sinks are scrubbed, and all cabinets and drawers are wiped down inside and outside. In bathrooms, all fixtures, including showers, bathtubs, toilets, and sinks, are cleaned and disinfected, and all mirrors and floors are thoroughly cleaned. Floors throughout the home are vacuumed and mopped, and all carpets are either vacuumed or steam-cleaned, depending on the service level. Move Out cleaning also involves cleaning windows, wiping down baseboards, and ensuring all areas, including closets and storage spaces, are dust-free and clean. This service helps you leave your old home with a clean slate, ensuring a smooth transition and the return of your security deposit if you're renting.

Post Renovation Service

Post Renovation cleaning is a crucial service designed to address the unique challenges that come with cleaning up after construction or renovation work. Renovation projects often leave behind a significant amount of dust, debris, and construction residue that can be difficult to clean up without professional help. Post Renovation cleaning focuses on thoroughly removing this dust and debris from all surfaces, including walls, ceilings, floors, and fixtures, to ensure that your newly renovated space is clean, safe, and ready for use. This service typically includes the removal of paint splatters, adhesive residues, and other construction-related stains; cleaning and polishing of all new fixtures and fittings; vacuuming and mopping of all floors; and detailed cleaning of windows, sills, and frames. The service also involves cleaning inside and outside of new cabinets, drawers, and closets, as well as ensuring that all appliances and surfaces are free from construction dust and debris. Post Renovation cleaning is ideal for homeowners, landlords, and businesses who want to enjoy their newly renovated space without the hassle of cleaning up after construction. This service helps to reveal the true beauty of your renovated space by leaving it spotless and ready for you to enjoy.

Custom Cleaning

Custom Cleaning is a highly personalized service that caters to your unique cleaning needs and preferences. Whether you require a focused clean on specific areas of your home or office, or you have particular tasks in mind that don't fit into standard cleaning packages, Custom Cleaning gives you the flexibility to create a cleaning plan that suits your exact requirements. This service allows you to choose from a wide range of cleaning tasks, from basic upkeep like dusting, vacuuming, and mopping, to more specialized tasks like deep cleaning of specific rooms, stain removal, window washing, and organization services. You can customize the frequency of the cleaning service, whether you need it on a one-time basis, weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly, and you can adjust the scope of work according to your needs. Custom Cleaning is ideal for clients who have specific priorities, such as preparing for a special event, cleaning after a large gathering, or focusing on particular areas like the kitchen, bathrooms, or guest rooms. By tailoring the cleaning service to your preferences, Custom Cleaning ensures that every corner of your space is cleaned to your satisfaction.
